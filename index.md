## Fourier transform
$$x(t) = c_0 + \sum_{n=1}^\infty A_n \cos(n\omega_0t + \phi_n)$$

Where $c_0$, $A_n$ and $\phi_n$ are based on the complex Fourier coefficients $c_n$.

~~~ matlab
y = fft(x)
~~~
